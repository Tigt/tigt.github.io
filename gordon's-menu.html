Bait

Name Description Price
Bait Can be used to fish, distract wild Pokemon with a Focus 12 DC check, or attract wild Pokemon by rolling 15 on a d20 every 15 minutes. (Bait used for fishing and attracting can be used 3 times before losing potency.) 150$ or 1 Honey

Tasty Snacks

If you don't like the taste of these, you still get the effect, but you're also Enraged.

Name Description Price
Salty Surprise When hit by an attack, gain 5 temporary HP. If you like Salty, gain 10 instead. 100$
Spicy Wrap When making a Physical attack, deal +5 damage for that attack. If you like Spicy, deal +10 instead. 100$
Sour Candy When hit by a Physical attack, gain +5 DR against that attack. If you like Sour, gain +10 instead. 100$
Dry Wafer When making a Special attack, deal +5 damage for that attack. If you like Dry, deal +10 instead. 100$
Bitter Treat When hit by a Special attack, gain +5 DR against that attack. If you like Sour, gain +10 instead. 100$
Sweet Confection Use at any time to gain +4 Evasion until the end of your next turn. If you like Sweet, also gain +4 Accuracy. 100$

Tastes

Gordon can add a Taste to any item he prepares. Tasty Snacks must have the same Taste as their basic flavor. As long as you don't dislike the Taste, you gain its effects alongside the main effect. It doesn't say, but I assume these effects last until the end of the Scene.

Name Description
Salty Gain 5 temporary HP. This stacks with other Chef features and Digestion Buffs.
Spicy Increase Critical Hit Range by +1.
Sour Increase Evasion against damaging attacks by +1.
Dry Increase Effect Range by +1.
Bitter +1 bonus to all Save Checks.
Sweet Increase Initiative by +5.

Complex Aftertaste

Whenever you use a Digestion Buff that had a Taste, Gordon can spend 1 AP to give you a new Digestion Buff that acts the same as a Tasty Snack of the same taste.
